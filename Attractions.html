Tower Bridge Guided Tour

Enjoy all the bridge has to offer, get up close and personal with iconic Towers, admire the spectacular views from the high-level Walkways and Glass Floors, then head down to the Engine Rooms, where the original Victorian Engines are on display.

Pricing Adult $55.68 - Child 16+yrs Child 5-15 yrs $47.44. The duration of the tour is around 1 hour to 90 minutes. Guided tours are also available which last around 90 minutes.
