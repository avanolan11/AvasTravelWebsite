Byward Kitchen and Bar London

The Byward Kitchen and Bar is a venue that offers homemade, traditional British food in a relaxing setting, near one of the country's favourite landmarks. Choose from their seasonal menu Breakfast, Lunch, evening supper or sample our London craft beer's or wine at the bar.

Prices range from $14-$39. Special diets include Vegeterian Friendly and Vegan Options.
